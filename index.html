SAFER SPACE is about creating a safer environment for everyone. There is no room for harassment, discrimination, boundary-crossing behaviour or unwanted attention.

No one can promise a completely safe space, but guests, crew and organisers can actively work to make it safer by taking warning signs and uncomfortable situations seriously.

What this means

What does Safer Space mean?

A safer space is an environment where everyone should be able to be present without fear of discrimination, unwanted touching, offensive comments or boundary-crossing behaviour.
