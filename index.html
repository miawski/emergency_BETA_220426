SAFER_SPACE

SAFER SPACE is about creating a safer environment for everyone. There is no room for harassment, discrimination, boundary-crossing behaviour or unwanted attention. A safer space is built on respect, consent and shared responsibility.

No one can promise a completely safe space, but we can actively work to make it safer. That means guests, crew and organisers help look after each other and take uncomfortable situations seriously.

What does Safer Space mean?

A safer space is an environment where everyone should be able to be present without fear of discrimination, unwanted touching, offensive comments or boundary-crossing behaviour.
